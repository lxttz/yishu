In this rapidly evolving digital age, conceptual art increasingly seems to be an obscure code that is intimidating to the public. It take sculpture, one of the representative forms of conceptual art, as the starting point, and combine it with the usual roller coaster in the amusement park to build a unique artistic experience. In this game, players can not only control the roller coaster independently, but also interact with various conceptual art works, and have the opportunity to freely display creativity on the artwork. 

Through this interactive experience, it hopes to arouse players' interest and understanding of postmodernist art in a new way, and provoke them to think deeply about the mutual influence and symbiotic relationship between contemporary art and popular culture.
