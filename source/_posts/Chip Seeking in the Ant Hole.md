This game interested in exploring the immersive experience of different perspectives shifting in virtual reality environment, and play with the idea of macro phobia and tropophobia, which are not very pleasant experiences to have, and make an adventure game that is fun and annoying at the same time. This is not only an adventure of seeking the mysterious chip, but also a challenging journey of exploring one’s fears and disturbing experiences, which echo with the sick feeling that remains a common issue unsolved with wearing vr headset.
