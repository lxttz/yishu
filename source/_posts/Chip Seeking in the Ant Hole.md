This project wanted to make an adventure game about exploring ant holes. The main character in the game has an important chip stolen by ants, so he needs a machine that can make him as small as ants and then dive into the ant hole to retrieve the chip. However, ant holes are dangerous, and if you touch some of the crystals belonging to the queen, you are likely to be eaten by the queen...



It is interested in exploring the immersive experience of different perspectives shifting in virtual reality environment, and play with the idea of macro phobia and tropophobia, which are not very pleasant experiences to have, and make an adventure game that is fun and annoying at the same time. This is not only an adventure of seeking the mysterious chip, but also a challenging journey of exploring one’s fears and disturbing experiences, which echo with the sick feeling that remains a common issue unsolved with wearing vr headset.
