In an era when the ocean is silently suffering from the trauma of human activity, Ethereal Tides is presented as a fusion of art and science. Created by Touch Designer, the interactive installation invites viewers to immerse themselves in a sensory journey that explores the fragile and delicate symbiotic relationship between ocean heatwaves, coral bleaching and marine life. The work is an intuitive narrative that transforms the plight of the oceans into a palpable experience, inspiring deeper reflection on ocean conservation.
