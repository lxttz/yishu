## About CHEN ANQI

Xing Chengming is an artist and researcher exploring the intersection of technology, art, and human experience. Through interactive installations, audio-visual works, and AI-driven systems, the practice investigates contemporary questions about memory, identity, and perception.

## Research Interests

- **AI & Machine Learning**: Exploring how artificial intelligence can transform visual expression and create new forms of artistic experience
- **Interactive Media**: Designing immersive environments where audience participation becomes an integral part of the work
- **Data Visualization**: Transforming complex datasets into meaningful visual narratives
- **Audio-Visual Art**: Creating synchronized multi-sensory experiences through sound and image

## Featured Works

### RAY
An intelligent system that translates human portraits into Rayographs using Conditional Adversarial Networks, exploring the changing ontology of images under machine gazes.

### A Walled City
An interactive AI installation that transforms personal and collective visual memories into a decentralized, evolving virtual city, grounded in the spatial logic of the historical Kowloon Walled City.

### Astro
An immersive audio-visual artwork examining the question of "why explore space" through the lens of an intelligent being in outer space, presented as a full-dome performance.
