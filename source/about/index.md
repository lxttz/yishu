Chen Anqi is an artist who is skilled at using dynamic images and space. She provides these new media mediums with more flexible options for expression. She is proficient in developing design based on the exploration process, where a critical perspective usually starts from the surrounding events or environment, drawing on a wide range of media forms and cultural research, forming a complex thinking network.

Her works focus on transforming creative concepts into captivating visual and interactive experiences, including visual visualization, layout design, 3D modeling and programming. This enables her works to possess the technical skills to bridge the gap between artistic conception and actual production. She has received numerous awards: the Visual Design Award at the ITCD - International Trend Design Competition (2022), the First Prize in the Visual Communication Category of the “Youth, Creativity, Power” Youth Creative Culture Competition (2023), IDA Design Award (2023), the Silver Award in the Concept Design Category at the London International Design Award (2024), the Bauhaus Modern Design Award (2024), and the CMF Visual Design Award (2025).

Anqi holds a Master’s degree in Computational Arts from Goldsmiths, University of London, and a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Communication Design from Nanjing Forestry University. While striving for updated visual effects, she also constantly guides her creations towards more research-oriented designs.
